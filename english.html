SayMore Emotion express combine art comforting

"Say More" creates an immersive and relaxing environment as an outlet for catharsis. Regardless of the emotions being positive or negative, through verbal expression and ink presentation, individuals transform their inner emotions and stress into artistic creations. The aim is for participants to perceive emotions from different perspectives, gain deeper insights into their emotional world, and courageously articulate their emotions, thereby achieving emotional health management and self-realization.
